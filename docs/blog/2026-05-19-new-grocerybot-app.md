Hi there - I'm excited to announce that GroceryBot now has an app!

With GroceryBot App, you are able to check your grocery list in one-click. No more navigating between Discord servers and channels just to get to your grocery list. Discord is heavy, whereas GroceryBot App is not.

_Read more below._

<!--truncate-->

It also comes with the ability to view your grocery list in Discord when you're offline. This is extremely useful for when you need to duck into a supermarket that has terrible signal reception. Combined with our lightweight design, looking at your grocery list is now as simple as, well, looking at your grocery list.

We're currently rolling this out to Android users **for free** (iOS coming soon!). In the future, we're planning to make the app experience exclusive to our Patreon members, so do sign-up there and support GroceryBot's development if you like the app!

You can install GroceryBot App here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.grocerybot.app.

For iOS users, please sign-up for the waitlist using GroceryBot's `/waitlist ios` command. We'll only send you an email* (yes, singular) once the app's been released on the App Store.

_*Why an email, you ask? Because I don't want to send people DMs through Discord (plus it's against Discord ToS if I remember correctly)._
